About Us

Welcome to the Heart Disease Indicator Dashboard, a project developed as part of a final year initiative to raise public awareness about heart disease. Our mission is to provide an accessible and user-friendly platform that helps individuals understand key health indicators associated with heart disease. This dashboard combines educational resources and interactive tools to empower users with the knowledge they need to make informed decisions about their heart health. By presenting critical data in a simple and engaging way, we aim to bridge the gap between medical information and everyday understanding.
